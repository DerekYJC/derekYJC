Beauty of tech city, Austin

As a capital of Texas, "tech city" Austin strikes balance between nature, eduation, the arts, and commerce. Due to its high quality of life, which attracts the best companies and talent over the world, Austin is one of the best place to live in America.

Summer break to Chicago

Chicago, the largest city in Illinois, provides world-class culture which is worth a visit. The breathtaking skyline, amazing museums, high-fashioned boutiques, and impressive architecture will be kept in mind forever once visiting here.

Explore Texans spirit in Dallas

Lone Star, so-called "Texan spirit", is the most special experience in Dallas. Barbecue, steaks, cowboy boots and live country music can be experienced in Stockyard. Let's become one-day cowboy and enjoy western lifestyle.

Astronaut dream comes true in Houston

As the biggest city in Texas, everything here is much much bigger, including dreams. Astronaut dream is fulfilled in Space Center that provides a great opportunity for space exploration.

Winter vacation to Florida

"The Sunshine State", Florida, is the most popular place to have a vacation. Spending whole vacation on visiting the megalopolis, Miami, the southest part of US, Keywest, national park, Everglades, and several amusement parks in Orlando is one of the best thing I have done during studying abroad.

Fredericksburg, German heritage in Texas

Fredericksburg is a historic town that provides a lesson in American history for Colonial and Civil War eras. It's worth a visit if you want to know more about the past of America.
